About Sign Engine

Welcome to Sign Engine, a revolutionary platform developed by Deimos Pvt Ltd for real-time sign language detection and translation. Powered by OpenCV, our cutting-edge technology enables accurate recognition and translation of sign language gestures, bridging communication gaps effortlessly

Technology Stack

Sign Engine is built using a powerful combination of state-of-the-art technologies, including:
  • Computer Vision: Utilizing OpenCV for real-time hand gesture recognition.

  • Machine Learning: Leveraging TensorFlow/Keras for accurate and efficient sign language translation.

  • WebRTC: For seamless video streaming and real-time processing.

  • Flask: Backend framework to manage server-side operations.

  • Streamlit: For creating an interactive and user-friendly web application.

    FAQ

    Our real-time sign language conversion feature uses advanced computer vision and machine learning technologies. The system captures video from your webcam and processes each frame to detect hand movements and gestures. These gestures are then matched to predefined sign language signs using a trained deep learning model. The recognized signs are converted into text and displayed in real time on the screen.

    If you notice that the sign language recognition is not accurate, please ensure that you have good lighting and that your hands are clearly visible in the webcam feed. Make sure there are no obstructions or background noise that might interfere with detection. Additionally, ensure that your hands are positioned within the camera's view and follow the gestures as accurately as possible. If issues persist, you can try recalibrating your webcam or reaching out to our support team for further assistance.

Regular updates and improvements to the underlying machine learning model also contribute to enhanced accuracy over time.
